Your Journey to Paris

In English

LitLife is an exploration of French literature and culture in a live online course format, taught in English, before you actually visit France as a small group—with your professor as your in-person guide.

Meet Jeff Kraft, your Professeur, Gentil-Organisateur

PGO is a fun way of saying I’ll be your teacher and guide. I’ve been to France more than 30 times and have taught college-level French language and culture for over a decade. I wrote and published the book Literary Paris.

LitLifeVentures is my next chapter: sharing my love and expertise to small groups online, then traveling to Paris together for an unforgettable week.
